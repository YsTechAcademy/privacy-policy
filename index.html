Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 04, 2026

Welcome to MathsG. Your privacy is paramount to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information within our mobile application. MathsG is built as a Frontend-Only educational tool.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) on any external servers. Our app is designed to function locally on your device. Specifically:

  • User Names: If you provide a name for the "Certificate of Completion," it is used solely for the local generation of that certificate.
  • Game Progress: Your stars, levels, and achievements are stored using local storage on your device.

2. Data Storage

All data generated within the app stays on your device. We do not have access to your progress, name, or any other activity. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, this information may be permanently deleted as we do not maintain backups on our end.

3. Third-Party Services

MathsG does not share, sell, or trade your data with any third-party companies. There are no hidden trackers or analytics tools embedded in the app that monitor your personal usage.

4. Children's Privacy

As an educational app, we are committed to protecting the privacy of children. Since we do not collect any data online, we are fully compliant with privacy standards for students and young learners.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
